Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my booking?

You can modify your booking through the "My Bookings" section up to 24 hours before departure.

What is the baggage allowance?

Economy class: 1 carry-on (7kg) + 1 checked bag (23kg). Business class: 2 carry-on + 2 checked bags (32kg each).

How do I get my boarding pass?

Boarding passes are available 24 hours before flight departure through "My Bookings" or at the airport check-in counter.
